January 14, 2026 — Mauritius

Strategic Dialogue with the Economic Development Board of Mauritius

During our exploratory business mission, we had the honour of a productive meeting with Mr. Mahen Abhimanu Kundasamy, CEO of the Economic Development Board (EDB) of Mauritius, alongside Ms. Padminee Chundunsing, President of the Canada–Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture, Mr. Daniel Paquette, Strategic Advisor, and their respective senior teams.

The Economic Development Board of Mauritius is the country's foremost institution for attracting and facilitating investment — a central interlocutor for any organization seeking to engage seriously with the Mauritian market. A meeting at this level reflects both the ambition of the Venture and the genuine openness of Mauritius to deepening its relationship with Canada.

Focused on Concrete Outcomes

The discussions were substantive and forward-looking. Key themes included:

  • Strengthening Canada–Mauritius trade and investment flows through coordinated institutional engagement
  • Strategic collaboration that drives innovation and economic growth in both countries
  • Building sustainable partnerships anchored in complementary strengths — Canadian expertise and capital, Mauritian market access and regional connectivity
  • The role of the Mauritius platform as a gateway to the broader African continent and Indian Ocean region

A High-Level Dialogue

This high-level dialogue marks another meaningful step in building the institutional foundations for a sustained Canada-Mauritius economic relationship. It reinforced the conviction that the conditions — and the will — are in place on both sides to move beyond conversation and into structured, durable partnership.

Together, the Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture and the EDB aim to create pathways for mutual prosperity — pathways that benefit businesses, communities and both nations over the long term.

For more information on investment and partnership opportunities across the Canada-Mauritius corridor, contact info@canadamauritius.com.
