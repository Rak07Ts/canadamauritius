Diaspora Engagement

Connecting Communities. Strengthening Canada–Africa and Canada–Mauritius Relations.

Our Approach

Diaspora Communities as Key Partners

Diaspora communities play a vital role in shaping strong, enduring relationships between countries. We are committed to engaging African diaspora communities in Canada, including the Mauritian diaspora, as key partners in fostering meaningful, people-to-people connections between Canada, Mauritius and the African continent more broadly.

Through dialogue, collaboration and community-driven initiatives, we recognize diaspora communities as cultural ambassadors, connectors and contributors to social, economic and civic life in Canada and across Africa.

Why Diaspora Engagement Matters

Diaspora engagement strengthens international relationships by:

  • Promoting mutual understanding and cultural awareness
  • Supporting community cohesion and inclusion in Canada
  • Leveraging lived experience, languages and cultural knowledge
  • Encouraging long-term collaboration beyond formal diplomacy
Our Objectives

What We Aim to Build

01

People-to-People Ties

Strengthen connections between Canada, Africa and Mauritius through sustained, community-driven engagement that goes beyond formal diplomacy.

02

Community Leadership

Support community leadership and participation among African-Canadian and Mauritian-Canadian communities across Canada.

03

Cultural Exchange

Promote cultural exchange, dialogue and shared understanding to preserve identity while fostering intercultural connection within Canada's diverse society.

04

Inclusive Spaces

Create inclusive spaces for connection, learning and shared identity across generations — for youth, elders and newcomers alike.

What We Do

Our Work in Practice

Community Engagement & Dialogue

We convene forums, roundtables and discussions that bring together members of African and Mauritian diaspora communities, community leaders, youth and partners to share perspectives and deepen collective understanding.

Cultural Connection & Celebration

We support and promote cultural initiatives that celebrate African and Mauritian heritage, histories and traditions — helping preserve cultural identity while fostering intercultural understanding.

Leadership & Capacity Building

Our work encourages diaspora leadership by supporting skills development, mentorship and participation in civic and community initiatives connected to Canada–Africa and Canada–Mauritius relations.

Intergenerational Engagement

We promote dialogue across generations, ensuring that youth, elders and newcomers all have opportunities to contribute to community life and international engagement.

Impact

What We Aim to Achieve

  • Build stronger, more connected communities
  • Increase awareness of Canada–Africa and Canada–Mauritius relations
  • Empower community voices and leadership
  • Support long-term collaboration rooted in trust and shared values

Diaspora engagement is not a one-time activity — it is a sustained commitment to inclusion, dialogue and relationship building.
