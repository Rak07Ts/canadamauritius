New Year's Eve with the Mauritian Diaspora in British Columbia

On December 31, 2025, members of the Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture gathered with the Mauritian diaspora community in British Columbia to welcome the New Year together — a warm and spirited evening that reflected the energy and connection at the heart of everything we do.

The event brought together Mauritians and friends of Mauritius from across British Columbia for a festive get-together rooted in shared heritage and a common vision. It was an occasion to reconnect, celebrate, and affirm the enduring bonds that link the Mauritian community in Canada across generations and geography.

More Than a Celebration

For the Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture, moments like this carry meaning beyond the occasion itself. The Mauritian diaspora in Canada is one of the most vibrant bridges between our two countries — a network of professionals, entrepreneurs and community leaders whose relationships and knowledge span both sides of the ocean.

This gathering in British Columbia was a reminder of the depth and breadth of that network, and of the genuine enthusiasm that exists within the community to support a stronger, more structured Canada-Mauritius relationship.

A Province with Growing Ties

British Columbia, with its dynamic business environment and a growing Mauritian community, represents an important part of the Venture's national footprint. Diaspora engagement across Canada — from BC to Ontario, from Québec to the Atlantic provinces — is central to how the Venture builds the human infrastructure of bilateral cooperation.

To learn more about diaspora engagement initiatives and how to get involved, reach out to info@canadamauritius.com.
