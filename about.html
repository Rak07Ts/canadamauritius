About Us

We exist to build lasting bridges between Canada and Mauritius.

Who We Are

We exist to drive prosperity

The Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture is a platform created to strengthen economic, institutional and people-to-people relations between Canada and Mauritius.

It supports the development of long-term relationships and targeted initiatives that can improve bilateral trade, encourage investment, increase visibility for both markets and connect relevant stakeholders across sectors.

The Venture is designed to operate at a practical level: facilitating introductions, highlighting opportunities, encouraging dialogue and helping structure a more coherent bilateral ecosystem.

Membership

Membership

Membership is intended for senior leaders, entrepreneurs, investors, institutional actors and strategic partners who are actively engaged in Canada-Mauritius economic relations or who wish to contribute to their development.

The Venture provides opportunities for membership, collaboration, sponsorship and strategic participation for organizations that see value in the Canada-Mauritius corridor.

Our Role

Our role is to create value for our members and partners, and more broadly for the bilateral relationship between Canada and Mauritius.

Our vision is to contribute to greater economic prosperity, stronger institutional linkages and more consistent engagement between both markets.

The Venture works with public, private and community stakeholders who have a role in fostering sustainable relations between Canada and Mauritius, including diaspora leaders, business associations, investors, entrepreneurs, educational actors and cultural advocates.

Insight

An expert body that advances business activity between Canada and Mauritius daily, providing strategic information and insight into trade and investment opportunities.

Intelligence

Working with researchers and advisors to deliver economic analysis, forecasting, studies and up-to-date information relevant to bilateral trade and investment.

Access

A premium network that continues to grow, ensuring high-level access in the public and private sectors for its members and partners.

Services

A facilitator of trade and investment: advisory services, introductions, promotional support, assistance for trade missions and high-level visits.

Leadership

The team driving the Canada-Mauritius Venture forward.

Padminee Chundunsing

President / Founder

Padminee Chundunsing provides strategic direction for the Venture and serves as a bridge-builder across business, community and Francophone networks. She is presented by the organization as a catalyst for inclusive growth and Francophone engagement, with experience in governance, advocacy and community leadership. Her wider public profile emphasizes consensus-building, bilingual leadership, cultural connection and long-term institution-building.

Cedric Medar

Director of Communications & Strategic Partnerships

Cedric Medar is a bilingual communications and marketing strategist focused on international relations, digital visibility and stakeholder engagement. His role supports partnership development, organizational positioning and the communication of Canada-Mauritius opportunities across audiences.

Mohammad Sheik Fareed

Director of Finance

Mohammad Sheik Fareed brings finance and government experience, with exposure to large portfolio oversight and international organizations. His contribution supports financial discipline, strategic planning and sustainable development thinking within the Venture's activities.

Daniel Paquette

Strategic Advisor

Daniel Paquette supports the strengthening of commercial relations between Mauritius and Canada. His advisory contribution focuses on market analysis, opportunity identification and practical strategies that can stimulate bilateral trade and investment.
