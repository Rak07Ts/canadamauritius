Focus Areas

Our work covers the sectors where Canada and Mauritius share the most potential for collaboration.

🏦

Trade & Investment

Facilitating trade flows, identifying investment opportunities and supporting market-entry strategies for businesses exploring the Canada-Mauritius corridor.

💰

Finance & Business Services

Exploring opportunities in financial services, fintech collaboration, and dual-market advisory and consulting services.

💻

Technology & Innovation

Supporting collaboration in digital transformation, technology transfer, startup ecosystems and smart-island solutions.

🌿

Renewable Energy & Sustainability

Promoting clean energy partnerships, climate resilience projects and sustainable development initiatives between both nations.

🎓

Education & Knowledge Exchange

Building bridges between educational institutions, fostering student exchange programs and supporting professional development partnerships.

✈️

Tourism & Hospitality

Connecting tourism operators, promoting destination awareness and supporting hospitality sector partnerships between Canada and Mauritius.

🚀

Entrepreneurship & SMEs

Supporting small and medium enterprises, fostering entrepreneurial collaboration and enabling cross-border business development.

🌍

Diaspora Engagement

Leveraging the Mauritian diaspora in Canada as a bridge for cultural, economic and institutional connection between the two countries.

🏗️

Infrastructure & Development

Identifying opportunities in infrastructure development, urban planning and construction partnerships that serve both markets.

Working Groups

Sector-specific collaboration

The Venture is organized around working groups that focus on specific sectors and thematic areas relevant to the bilateral relationship.

These working groups are composed of members and invited experts who meet periodically to identify opportunities, discuss trends, share market intelligence and develop collaborative initiatives.

Working groups currently under development include:

  • Trade & Market Access
  • Finance & Investment
  • Technology & Innovation
  • Education & Knowledge Exchange
  • Diaspora & Cultural Bridge-Building
  • Sustainability & Climate Action

Want to contribute to a focus area?

If you have expertise or interest in any of these areas, we welcome your engagement.
