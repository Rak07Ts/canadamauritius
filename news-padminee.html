Leadership Spotlight:
Padminee Chundunsing

Padminee Chundunsing is the President and Founder of the Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture — a bilingual bridge-builder whose work spans governance, community leadership, Francophone network development and international business engagement.

She founded the Venture with a clear and practical belief: that the Canada-Mauritius relationship, though rich in shared history, values and diaspora connection, had never been fully structured for sustained economic engagement. Her goal was to change that — to build a corridor that serves not just major institutions, but entrepreneurs, diaspora leaders, students, and anyone with a genuine stake in a stronger bilateral future.

A Catalyst for Bilateral Progress

Padminee's profile reflects a consistent thread across different roles and contexts: building consensus, fostering dialogue and creating durable connections across cultural and linguistic boundaries. She brings deep familiarity with Francophone networks in Canada, longstanding community leadership experience and a practical understanding of the challenges and opportunities specific to the Canada-Mauritius space.

What sets her work apart is her insistence on structure and continuity. Where others have treated the Canada-Mauritius relationship as an opportunity for occasional networking, Padminee has built an organization designed for the long term — with a leadership team, a membership structure, working groups, a business mission and a growing public presence.

"The Canada-Mauritius relationship has a real foundation: shared Commonwealth ties, democratic institutions, bilingual environments and a diaspora that has always served as a living bridge. Our role is to formalize what has always been possible — to add structure, visibility and continuity to that connection."

— Padminee Chundunsing, President & Founder

Building the Organization

Under her leadership, the Venture moved with intention and speed. In the space of a few months, it established its public identity, introduced its leadership team, led its first exploratory business mission in January 2026 and positioned itself as a credible platform for structured Canada-Mauritius engagement.

The Venture's approach reflects Padminee's emphasis on quality over quantity: a selective membership model, a focused set of priority sectors, and a commitment to advancing concrete bilateral outcomes rather than simply convening conversations.

Francophone Leadership and Cultural Connection

One of the distinctive dimensions of Padminee's leadership is her emphasis on the Francophone dimension of the Canada-Mauritius relationship. Both Canada and Mauritius are Francophone nations — members of the Organisation internationale de la Francophonie — and this shared linguistic and cultural heritage provides a powerful, often underutilized, basis for bilateral cooperation.

Her ability to operate across English and French-speaking environments, and to connect Francophone communities on both sides of the relationship, gives the Venture an important advantage in reaching stakeholders that purely Anglophone approaches would miss.

Looking Ahead

Padminee Chundunsing's vision for 2026 and beyond was ambitious and grounded. It included expanding the Venture's membership, building active working groups in key sectors, hosting bilateral events in Canada and Mauritius, and continuing to advocate for stronger institutional representation — including a permanent diplomatic presence to support the growing relationship.

Her wider work as a community leader, advocate and entrepreneur continues to inform the Venture's values: inclusive, bilingual, long-term in its thinking, and committed to creating genuine opportunity for the people and organizations it serves.
