Membership

The Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture provides membership and sponsorship opportunities to organizations and leaders interested in the Canada and Mauritius markets.

Membership is selective and intended for senior decision-makers, founders, investors, institutional representatives and recognized professionals whose work can contribute to a stronger bilateral relationship.

Become a Member

If you are interested in becoming part of the Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture, please contact info@canadamauritius.com with "Membership" in the subject line.

Membership discussions should focus on alignment with the organization's mission, the quality of the applicant's contribution to the bilateral ecosystem, and the ability to engage constructively in long-term initiatives.

Sponsorship

Organizations are invited to enquire about sponsoring existing or collaborative events, business delegations, briefings, networking forums, roundtables and future resource publications.

For sponsorship opportunities, please contact info@canadamauritius.com with "Sponsorship" in the subject line.
