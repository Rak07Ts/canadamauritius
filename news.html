January 12–15, 2026

Exploratory Business Mission from Canada to Mauritius

This exploratory mission is designed to engage government representatives, private-sector stakeholders, investors and members of the Canadian diaspora in Mauritius in order to identify strategic opportunities and support sustainable long-term partnerships.

The mission reflects Canada's broader objective to diversify its global economy through practical bilateral engagement.

Introducing the Canada Mauritius Leadership Team

The Venture has introduced its founding leadership team, highlighting expertise across governance, communications, finance and market development.

Advocacy Focus: Establishing a Mauritian Embassy in Canada

The organization has publicly positioned the establishment of a Mauritian embassy in Canada as a strategic step toward stronger bilateral relations.

The advocacy case emphasizes stronger diplomatic and economic ties, improved support for Mauritian citizens in Canada, and new bridges for trade, tourism, education, innovation and culture.
