About

Canada and Mauritius are well positioned to deepen a high-value bilateral relationship.

Canada and Mauritius share strong foundations for long-term cooperation: democratic institutions, Commonwealth ties, bilingual and multicultural environments, and a growing interest in innovation-led, globally connected growth.

Mauritius offers a strategic gateway into Africa and the Indian Ocean region, with strengths in financial services, logistics, tourism, education, renewable energy and international business. Canada offers deep capabilities in investment, technology, clean growth, infrastructure, education, advanced services and diaspora-driven entrepreneurship.

The opportunity now is to transform this connection into a more structured and visible corridor for trade, investment, knowledge exchange and business development.

The Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture

The Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture is a Canada-based initiative dedicated to strengthening bilateral economic ties between Canada and Mauritius. It brings together business leaders, investors, institutions, entrepreneurs and community stakeholders to build practical bridges across both markets.

The Venture supports dialogue, facilitation, visibility and continuity in the way bilateral opportunities are identified and advanced.

Its purpose is not only to celebrate the Canada-Mauritius relationship, but to help convert that relationship into meaningful projects, partnerships and long-term shared prosperity.

Our Vision

Our vision is to help build a thriving Canada-Mauritius partnership that promotes sustainable progress, knowledge exchange, inclusive opportunity and long-term economic cooperation.

Our Mission

The Canada Mauritius Trade and Investment Venture is a business-led platform that promotes bilateral trade, investment and strategic collaboration between Canada and Mauritius.

Our mission is to facilitate relationships and initiatives that improve bilateral market accessibility, strengthen institutional and private-sector engagement, and increase high-quality trade and investment opportunities.
